I am a senior undergraduate student majoring in computer science at the University of Science and Technology of China, now actively seeking a master's degree in computer science in the United States.

My research interest is an intersection of **computer vision, multimedia, and natural language processing**, with a particular emphasis on **multi-modal generative AI**. My undergraduate thesis is about cross-modal retrieval, and my previous research and internship experience involve talking-head generation and RAG (retrieval-augmented generation) applications. I am currently conducting research under the supervision of Professor [Chenyu You](https://chenyuyou.me/) at Stony Brook University, where I am developing a general multimodal biomedical generative model for synthesizing medical images across various organs and imaging modalities based on textual descriptions, with applications in clinical settings.

I am also a dedicated student athlete, with seven university records in swimming and multiple golds in various track and field events. I won a seventh place at the Chinese University Swimming Championships in 2023. Now I am attempting to run a marathon and hopefully finish a triathlon. I also love to travel around the world!
